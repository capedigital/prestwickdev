Expand Your Students' Literary Horizons

When it comes to literature, teaching the classics is important. But it's never a bad idea to try something different once in a while. Adding a title from the last ten years, a contemporary novel written by a woman or person of color, or a book that connects with themes from current events may help your students see how relevant literature can be. That's why we're always on the lookout for new books to share with your students.
