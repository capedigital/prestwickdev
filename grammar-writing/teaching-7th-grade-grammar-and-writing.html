Maximum Impact

Caught’ya! Grammar with a Giggle

Caught’ya! Grammar with a Giggle

Jane Bell Kiester’s books are a great alternative to skill-and-drill grammar practice and a creative way to generate enthusiasm for learning. Using classroom-proven soap opera plots, students are given a sentence riddled with grammar mistakes. As students work to correct grammar as quickly as possible, the teacher moves among them and calls out “Caught’ya!” when a student has missed an error. Each book includes answer keys, plot outlines, and spin-off activities.

Sentence Composing for Middle School
