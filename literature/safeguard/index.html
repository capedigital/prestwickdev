Safeguard™ Editions

Keep your beloved paperbacks secure for a lifetime with our Safeguard™ hardcovers.

Great literature endures, and so should your books.

How many books are destroyed by your students each year? Tired of wasting school money on your worn-out copies of Catcher in the Rye year after year?

We can help. Students may be tough on books, but Safeguard™ hardcover literature books stand up to even the most brutal treatment. Give your books the quality care they deserve - and give yourself the peace of mind that comes with using sturdy Safeguard bindings on all of your favorite paperbacks.

Prestwick House Safeguard™ editions:

  • Extend the life of the book with a lifetime guarantee
  • Help you save money long term - no more replacing your books!
  • Are always manufactured in the USA

We make superior quality bindings that are reliable and will stand the test of time. That's why Prestwick House is proud to offer lifetime protection on hardcover editions of all your favorite books.

Safeguard™ Edition Lifetime Guarantee

Safeguard Edition Bindings meet the LBI library binding standards and are sold with a lifetime guarantee against binding wear and breakage in the normal course of usage. This binding guarantee is against binding failure and damage in the normal course of usage and does not include willful damage of that of gross negligence (including but not limited to burning, water damage, intentional destruction, etc.).

Simply return damaged books to Prestwick House with return postage, and we'll send you replacement books as soon as they're available.
