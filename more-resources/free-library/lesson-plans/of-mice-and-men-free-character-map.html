Of Mice and Men Free Character Map

John Steinbeck’s Of Mice and Men remains one of the most taught books in classrooms today. Taking place on a small ranch in California at the height of the Great Depression, this powerful story explores themes relating to isolation, friendship, and the pursuit of the American Dream.

As they read the novella, have students fill out this character map to keep track of the ties between George, Lennie, Curley, and other characters.
