The Dark Side of Literature: Dracula Free Poster

Bram Stoker’s best-known work of spell-binding horror, Dracula, is immortalized with this free poster! Stoker’s classic novel brings forward many topics of discussion, from the dichotomy between modernity and ancient teachings to viewing the female form as a sexual weapon. Written in an epistolary format from the point of view of multiple characters, Dracula perfectly encompasses both a supernatural thriller and a murder-mystery.
