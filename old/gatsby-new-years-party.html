What are Literary Touchstone Classics?

Prestwick House Literary Touchstone Classics are unabridged, complete texts of beloved works that are designed with the needs of both teachers and students in mind.

Each book is a value-priced, high-quality trade paperback that’s packed with valuable material to enhance students’ understanding of the text, including brief introductions and author information, reading pointers for sharper insights, and glossary and vocabulary lists.
