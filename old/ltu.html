Why spend hours planning a literature unit from scratch?

Created by seasoned educators using an organized academic framework, Prestwick House Literature Teaching Units are comprehensive lesson plans for more than 300 different book titles. Literature Teaching Units are ready to use right away, saving you hours of traditional prep work.

Prestwick House Literature Teaching Units are best used to teach students who are performing at grade level or in College Prep classes. For more challenging resources, browse our AP Literature Teaching Units.

See the full list of available titles

Each Literature Teaching Unit includes:

  • Learning objectives that your students should meet by the time they finish the unit
  • Literary terms with definitions that your students will encounter when studying the book
  • Vocabulary lists for each section of the book
  • Essay and discussion questions to drive interesting classroom conversations or make assigning a paper easy
  • A multiple-choice and essay test to make a quick, objective assessment of student understanding
  • A chapter-by-chapter reproducible study guide with open-ended questions to guide students to a thorough understanding of the book
