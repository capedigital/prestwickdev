Want to celebrate National Book Month with your students? Share these exciting and engaging activities with them!

Connect with Your Favorite Authors

Many authors love to hear from their readers, especially students. Find your favorites on social media or consider reaching out via email.

Recommend Your Favorite Book to a Friend

Split into small groups and tell your friends about a book you loved—maybe you’d even be willing to lend it to them!

Draw Character Maps

Connect the dots between characters in the book you’re currently reading by making a character map. Need inspiration? Check out our latest free map for Frankenstein.

Plot Your Own Book Ideas

Ever wanted to write your own novel? Using the standard narrative structure, write a quick summary of the book that’s in your imagination!
