Reading is the very basis for modern civilization. From understanding a bus schedule to reading scientific documents, to participating in social media, students are exposed to more reading today than they ever were before.

With Prestwick House reading programs, your students will engage in active learning, using both fiction and nonfiction, to improve their reading comprehension.
