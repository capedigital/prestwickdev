Get students excited for reading with dozens
of creative activities for literature.

Show your students how enjoyable studying literature can be with Prestwick House Activity Packs. These title-specific, reproducible packets contain plenty of literature-based activities that help students think critically while learning about literary elements such as theme, symbolism, characterization, allusion, and more.

Activity Packs are the perfect way to get your students engaged in literary analysis - even those reluctant to like literature.

Each Activity Pack contains more than 40 different activities, including:

  • Writing poetry
  • Creating dramatizations
  • Completing maps and charts
  • Creating collages
  • Conducting surveys
  • Staging sets and scenes
  • Devising scenarios
  • Drawing editorial cartoons
