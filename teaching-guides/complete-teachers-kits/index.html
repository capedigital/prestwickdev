Complete Teacher's Kits

Get everything you need to teach a new book with three literature teaching resources in one package.

Add variety to your lessons with three approaches to learning.

Whether your students learn best through a traditional approach, by completing fun and interesting activities, or by crafting personal responses to literature, Complete Teacher's Kits give you the resources to reach all members of the class. Every title-specific Complete Teacher's Kit contains a Teaching Unit, an Activity Pack, and a Response Journal. Select print titles also come with Headlines posters.

Plus, when you buy a Complete Teacher's Kit with a 30 paperback set, we'll automatically upgrade those paperbacks to our biggest discount - 35% off!

View All Complete Teacher's Kits

Complete Teacher's Kits come with these great literature resources:

Download a Sample
  • Literature Teaching Units include a chapter-by-chapter study guide with short-answer questions, a test with multiple-choice and essay questions, a detailed list of learning objectives, and essential background information about the text.
  • Activity Packs are filled with dozens of activities that will help your students explore literary elements like theme, symbolism, character, figurative language, allusion, and more.
  • Response Journals are packed with writing prompts that will encourage your students to reflect on the text and connect it to their own lives.
