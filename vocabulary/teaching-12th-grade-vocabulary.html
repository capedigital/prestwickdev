Want a comprehensive vocabulary program for your 12th-grade students? Look no further than Prestwick House. Our range of programs uses rigorous exercises and engaging activities to help your high school seniors build their vocabularies and gain valuable language arts skills.

Vocabulary Power Plus

12th Grade

Class Set Individual Copy

Vocabulary Power Plus for College and Career Readiness

Prepare your class for the SAT, ACT, and Common Core assessments with Vocabulary Power Plus for College and Career Readiness - Level 12. This program is designed to give your 12th-grade students weekly vocabulary practice and expose them to the question formats that they will encounter on standardized tests and beyond.

With exercises that help students use words in context and make inferences, new two-part multiple-choice questions, and revised writing prompts and reading passages, Vocabulary Power Plus will give your students a leg up on every element of language arts.

  • Crepuscular
  • Incongruous
  • Rescind
  • Serendipity
  • And 310 more!
