Keep vocabulary instruction simple.

Vocabulary Power Plus Online is completely browser-based - no software download required. All you need is an internet connection. Each course of the program is composed of 21 lessons. A simple interface makes navigating through each lesson, segment, and activity a breeze.

Pique student interest with interactive lessons.

Forget giving your students boring worksheets and static word lists—engage them with interactive assessments that help them actively learn. The dynamic content within each lesson reinforces students’ acquisition and use of new words through a range of practice activities and assessments.

Encourage deeper learning with instant feedback.

Practice makes perfect, and with Vocabulary Power Plus Online, students know exactly what they need to study. Instant feedback in each lesson guides students toward word mastery. After all, practice is more fun for students when they know what they get right.

Save valuable instruction time in the classroom.

Unlike other edtech tools, Vocabulary Power Plus Online is easy to implement in your curriculum without requiring a lot of in-class management. Once your class is enrolled, you can let your students work through lessons at whatever pace makes sense for you. There are no confusing calendars, complicated assignments, or time-consuming grading to worry about.

See grades at a glance.

Say goodbye to spending hours grading student work. Vocabulary Power Plus Online automatically grades every assessment, giving you an instant view of how students are doing at any moment. Complete with time-on-task data and color-coded scoring, the built-in gradebook lets you track student progress with ease.

Improve skills with techniques based on proven methodology.

Vocabulary Power Plus Online helps students learn and retain more than 300 words per level through careful repetition, instant feedback, and a variety of question types that build rigor, from matching definitions to careful usage questions.
